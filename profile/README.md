# BIOSC 1640: Computational Biology Research

Undergraduate capstone course for Computational Biology majors at the [University of Pittsburgh](https://www.pitt.edu/), where student teams build production-grade computational biology pipelines to address open research questions.

Real problems. Real data. Real stakeholders. Every project lives here.

---

**Want to sponsor a project?** We seek collaborations with faculty, postdocs, and industry partners. Contact Dr. Alex Maldonado at <alex.maldonado@pitt.edu> to schedule a scoping meeting.
This course is only offered in Spring semesters (January through April).
